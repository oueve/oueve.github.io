Basics

Here's what you'll need to know to use Orueve!


Water tracking made easy.



Haven't been drinking a lot of water lately? Oueve is an iOS app that allows you to monitor your drinking habits in a single, unified space. You'll be able to tell if you need to drink more water easily.




Track your progress.




Your daily total and the recommended water you should drink are on the homescreen, right where you need it.
