Mission Statement

Here at Oueve, we're committed to keeping you healthy. That's why we've created oueve, an app that makes sure that you stay hydrated.


Our team


Oueve started as a simple idea to track water consumption in 2016 and quickly grew to an idea that aimed to help its users stay hydrated and to maintain a healthy lifestyle. With features like Smart Reminders and Oueve Assistant, Oueve is optimized to provide helpful advice and insight on improving water consumption.
